One of the biggest challenges for people with osteoarthritis is communicating their activities and pain frequency to their doctor. PainOA helps streamline the communication between doctor and patient by tracking these daily metrics.

PainOA will also provide personalized insights and feedback to help you understand how your health behaviors can influence your osteoarthritis symptoms.

More generally,patients with osteoarthritis may ultimately benefit from using the PainOA platform because it provides inciteful and indepth data points for your doctor to monitor therefore allowing this to provide personalized patient care.
