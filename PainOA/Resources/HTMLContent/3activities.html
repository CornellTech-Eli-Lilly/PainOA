PainOA provides personalized insights into how your health behaviors can help you manage your osteoarthritis symptoms. In general, richer data entered into the app results in more accurate and informative insights.

To drive these insights, PainOA will ask you to:

  • Answer questions about your type osteoarthritis and health behaviors (described more in a subsequent screen)
  • Record your activity
  • Record the frequency and location of your pain

The app sends occasional reminders to complete study activities.
