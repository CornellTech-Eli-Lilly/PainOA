About this Study

Welcome to PainOA!

PainOA helps you keep track of health behaviors important for people with osteoarthritis, such as physical activity, hours of sleep, pain intensity and taking you pain medicines. Data that you share through the app will help your physician manage your pain management schedule. The app provides personalized insights into how often you are physically active and helps you manage your osteoarthritis while increasing the efficiency of communcation with your doctor.
