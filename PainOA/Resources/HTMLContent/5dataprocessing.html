PainOA helps you keep track of health behaviors important for people with osteoarthritis, such as physical activity, pain levels, and taking your medicines. Data that you share through the app as part of the research study will create an unprecedented crowd-sourced database of health behaviors and glucose values from people just like you. Studying all this real-world data will help doctors create a personalized pain treatment plan.

At the same time, PainOA analyzes your data to provide personalized insights into how your diet and physical activity relate to your osteoarthritis pain levels, and helps you manage your type osteoarthritis.

By combining a personal app and a platform for the doctors to receive comprehensive patient data, PainOA will help explore how the iPhone can enable new kinds of clinical research.
